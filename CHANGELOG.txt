CDeTDS — Changelog
==================

v1.0.4 (June 2026) - IT Act 2025 transition audit, payment-code groundwork, UI fixes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TRANSITION CORRECTNESS (CRITICAL — 1961 Act -> 2025 Act)
  * Trigger-date rule: TDS entry now has a Credit Date (books) field; the governing
    Act/FY/Quarter is derived from the EARLIER of credit date and payment date,
    never the session FY. A Mar-2026-credited / Apr-2026-paid entry stays in the
    old-Act (FY 2025-26) stream as required.
  * Returns page form dropdown is FY-aware: TY 2026-27+ offers Form 138/140/143
    (was 24Q/26Q/27EQ); selection re-maps automatically on FY switch.
  * Mixing validation: generation blocks an old form on a new-act FY (and vice
    versa) with a friendly message naming the correct form.
  * Paper return is FY-aware: "FORM NO. 138 (erstwhile Form 24Q)", Income-tax
    Act 2025 citations (s.392/393/394), "TY" year label, section 395 note.
  * Assessment Year abolished under Act 2025: top bar / dashboard show "TY 2026-27"
    (= financial year); AY still shown for FY 2025-26 and earlier.
  * TDS entry section field shows the Act 2025 reference (e.g. "Files as Section
    393(1) Sl.6") for new-act FYs while keeping the familiar 194-series code.
  * Report/certificate footers now cite the correct Act based on the document FY
    (was hardcoded "Income-tax Act 2025").

IT ACT 2025 PAYMENT CODES (future-ready, OFF by default)
  * New tds_rules.payment_code column + per-rule editor field + activation switch
    on Rules Status. Codes staged from Protean RPU v1.0 BETA (1001-1092); files
    keep the FVU 9.4 interim format until the FINAL new-act FVU/RPU is released
    and codes are re-verified. Bundled the official FVU/RPU v1.0 (Beta) artifacts.

DATA SAFETY
  * Single-instance guard (mutex) prevents two app instances corrupting the DB.
  * Filing snapshots: a record-level copy of each generated statement is stored
    for future correction statements.
  * Per-challan reconciliation check in return pre-validation (Sum of linked
    entries vs challan amount).
  * Multi-statement DB writes (filing history, FVU config) wrapped in transactions.

TDS RULES
  * Fixed Act attribution on FY 2025-26 rules (were mislabelled "IT Act 2025";
    now "IT Act 1961"); installed DBs self-heal on startup.
  * Fixed 194Q mislabelled "Removed" (it continues; 206AB was removed).
  * Section Guide and Rules Status are FY-aware (act name, year label, Finance
    Act year, Act 2025 reference column).
  * FY aggregate threshold (194C Rs.1,00,000) tracked across the year.
  * Late-deposit interest warning (s.201(1A)) on entry save.

UI / DOCS
  * Fixed page content being clipped/unreachable at the bottom (flex-shrink) and
    restored a thin scrollbar on the main content area.
  * Help drawer: new "IT Act 2025" category (8 entries) + single-instance entry.
  * README / FAQ corrections (DB filename, paths, Act references).

TESTS
  * 311 tests pass (added transition, mixing-validation, paper-return, AY/TY,
    aggregate-threshold, and report PDF smoke tests).
